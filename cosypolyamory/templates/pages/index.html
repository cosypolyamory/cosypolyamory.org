Cosy Polyamory Community

Who we are

We are a welcoming space for people exploring polyamory / ethical non monogamy mostly based in Barcelona.

Our values

Transparency

Fairness

Equality

We encourage members to participate in ways that feel comfortable—whether that’s attending events, sharing ideas, or helping to create a cosy, inclusive atmosphere.

Becoming a member of the community

Please sign up on our Meetup page. Once you’ve joined, you’ll receive a welcome email with our Code of Conduct. Read the Code of Conduct fully and then answer a few simple questions to confirm that you've read it.

Once approved, you’ll gain access to our Meetup group, where you can RSVP to events and meet members. After attending an event, the organizer may invite you to join our main Telegram Community Chat. Meeting in person helps us ensure a safe and welcoming environment for all.

If you have questions, please connect with our team to ask about our policies, or learn more about upcoming events.

We’re excited to meet you and welcome you to our cosy community!
