Welcome to the

Cosy Polyamory Community!

We are a welcoming space for people exploring polyamory / ethical non-monogamy mostly based in Barcelona. Our community hosts events where you can learn more about polyamory, meet like-minded individuals, and build connections in a safe and friendly environment.

Our core values are fairness, equality, and transparency. We encourage members to participate in ways that feel comfortable—whether that’s attending events, sharing ideas, or helping to create a cosy, inclusive atmosphere. Together, we strive to support one another and say yes to opportunities that bring us closer as a community.

Our events include social gatherings, book clubs, hikes, beach outings, and more, mostly in Barcelona. We’re always open to new ideas, and members are encouraged to propose events that interest them.

Becoming a member of the community

Please read our Code of Conduct and then apply to become a member of our community. You'll need to answer a few simple questions to confirm that you've read the Code of Conduct.

Once approved, you’ll gain full access to this site, where you can find out about upcoming events. After attending an event, the organizer may invite you to join our main Telegram Community Chat. Meeting in person helps us ensure a safe and welcoming environment for all

If you have questions, please connect with our team to ask about our policies, or learn more about upcoming events.

We’re excited to meet you and welcome you to our cosy community!
